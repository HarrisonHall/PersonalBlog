  • My library

    Updated September 2017

    One of my fundamental beliefs is that the core human motive is growth. I know it is mine. Without growth in my education or work I find myself feeling unsatisfied. If I'm not busy, I'm wasting time. I guess I sort of believe in Parkinson's law in that I will be busy no matter what. If I have 6 clubs and 6 classes I will be just as busy as if I had 2 clubs and 4 classes. That's why I organize my days the way I do. I try to read whenever I have a chance. The library below consists of books I've read recently that I feel have had a fundamental impact on my own development: mentally, spiritually, or academically.
