The Trial

  • The original text-based game

    Introducing the original text-based adventure game created by Harrison Hall, and Jacky Wong, featuring art from Olga Suchankova. The Trial was created in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript and feautures a complex branch system to allow you to make your own decisions.

    The Trial stars you as the main character in deciding the fate of your hometown, Nubatormenta, as an outworldly ship lingers in the distance.
